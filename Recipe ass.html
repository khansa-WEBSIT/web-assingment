Mouth Watering Lava Cake

Here you can see the easiest recipe of lava cake

Ingredients

  1. butter 4tbsp
  2. semi sweet chocolate chips 1/3cup
  3. icing sugar 1/2cup
  4. egg yolk 1
  5. egg 1
  6. all purpose flour 3tbsp
  7. vanilla extract 1/4tsp

Direction

  • Preheat oven at 200 C
  • In a bowl add butter,semi-sweet chocolate chips and microwave it for 30-45 seconds then mix well
  • Stir in the sugar until well blended
  • Add egg yolk and egg,whisk well
  • Stir in flour,mix well
  • Add vanilla extract and give it a good mix
  • Greese your ramekins or bowl
  • Add some flour or coco powder and dust excess
  • Add mixture in ramekins/bowls
  • Add bake at 200C for exact 14 minutes
  • Cake should have firm side and the center remains soft
  • Let stand for a minute and invert the cake on separate plate while it`s warm
  • Sprinkle icing sugar and serve
